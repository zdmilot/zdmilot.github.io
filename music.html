Music

I have been involved in music for over 10 years now, and will continue to pursue for the rest of my life. I remember picking up the alto saxophone for the first time and immediately realizing that music would be my passion for the rest of my life.

Backround

The first time I played the alto saxophone I fell in love. The way it felt, and the fact that it seemed so natural for me to play was incredible. Through grade school I improved on my musical abilities trough playing classical and jazz music in my schools band program. The experiences here would visibly transcend far into my future.

Jump into Jazz

During grade school I was introduced into jazz music and immediately knew that it is where I wanted to be. Jazz was able to be, from what I saw, a more creative way of expressing music. The ability to fluidly improvise and change and innovate melodies was what most appealed to me. This surly required more talent and a different mindset, than I possessed in grade school. This caused me to participate in numerous jazz band camps and music lessons to try and help improve my skill. Jazz continues to be a challenge in which I love trying to conquer every time I play.

BVT Jam Band

A passion for the arts is much better when shared. When I was accepted into the vocational high school I'm in now they did not have a band. My passion and love was music so I knew I must help create and foster a program to promote and improve the arts. I created the BVT Jam Band with one of my fellow peers and some members of the school's faculty. This program has developed immensely over the year and a half it has been running and it continues to grow with every practice. I feel that if I helped at least one person retain their musical background I have done what I came to the school to do.
