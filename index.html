Zachary Milot's Portfolio

Marine Science Major at Boston University

Born in Milford, Massachusetts, Zachary Milot gained his high school diploma at Blackstone Valley Regional Vocational Technical High School in Upton Massachusetts majoring in Information Technology, Networking, and Telecommunications in 2015. During high school, he was a part of the First Robotics team as the Secretary of the Software Division, partook in the school’s Cooperative Education Program by interning at TMC Environmental as an Information Systems Intern, and created and lead the school’s first band. On top of all that he joined a program in which ambitious students had the chance to take college-level courses at Massachusetts Maritime Academy in both Oceanography and Aquaculture. Once graduated he decided to pursue a degree in Electrical Engineering at the University of Massachusetts, Lowell. After a semester in he decided that his true passion lied in the Marine Sciences.
