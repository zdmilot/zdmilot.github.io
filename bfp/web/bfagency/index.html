About

We are incorporated and licensed in the State of Massachusetts. With over 30 years of combined experience in the fire protection industry, our staff is fully licensed and highly trained. As a licensed fire sprinkler contractor, our services include: design, calculations, testing, repair, retrofitting and installation of fire sprinkler systems. In order to ensure that we are a part of the development of the latest standards, initiatives and innovations, we are involved with a number of key industry organizations . This allows us to provide the best and most up to date services to our customers.
